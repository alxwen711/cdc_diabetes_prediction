# Code of Conduct

The purpose of this code of conduct is to allow contributors to this project to work together in a manner that is safe and respectful for all.

## Diversity and inclusivity

- We believe a diversity of perspectives from people with a diversity of backgrounds and life experience can create better, more creative solutions than otherwise would be possible.
- All people are welcome to contribute to this project regardless of: race, skin colour, other genetic characteristics, age, religion, nationality, sex, sexual orientation, gender idenity, gender expression, family status, disability, etc.

## Expected and unacceptable behavior

- All contributers will be treated with respect and dignity.
- All contributers will have an opportunity to be heard, to challenge existing ideas, and to propose new ones.
- Disagreements will be discussed respectfully.
- No mini-groups will be created and no one will be left out of group discussion.

### Examples of unacceptable behavior

- excluding contributers from meetings/discussion
- not listening to everyone's ideas
- rudeness
- name calling/put downs

## Consequences for unacceptable behavior

Unacceptable behavior will lead to a team meeting, if unaccpetable behavior continues it will lead to a the perpetrator being reported to MDS faculty.

## Procedure for reporting and dealing with unacceptable behavior

1. A summary of the unacceptable behavior will be created with contribution from all contributers who witnessed the behaviour.
2. The summary will be sent to a member of MDS faculty.
